About our Community

Nestled in the heart of Alberta, Taber is a community that embodies the warmth and friendliness for which small towns are renowned. Residents of Taber take pride in their tight-knit community, where a strong sense of camaraderie is fostered through shared experiences and mutual support.

The town boasts well-maintained parks that serve as vibrant hubs for recreation and relaxation. Families often gather in these green spaces, creating a lively atmosphere filled with laughter and the joy of community connection. Taber's commitment to maintaining beautiful parks reflects the town's dedication to providing residents with a high quality of life.

From tranquil walking paths to playgrounds echoing with the laughter of children, these public spaces contribute to the town's charm. As the sun sets over the picturesque landscapes, residents of Taber can often be found enjoying the serenity of their community, grateful for the unique blend of natural beauty and welcoming neighborliness that defines this delightful town.

Fun facts

Sugar Beet Capital

In addition to corn, Taber is also known for its sugar beet production. The town has historically been a major center for sugar beet farming in Canada.

Historic Downtown

Taber has a charming and historic downtown area with unique shops and businesses. It offers a glimpse into the town's history and showcases its community spirit.

Taber Cornfest

The Taber Cornfest is an annual event that celebrates the town's agricultural heritage. It features live entertainment, a parade, a classic car show, and, of course, plenty of delicious corn.
